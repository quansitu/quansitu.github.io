I am now a post-doc in Université Clermont Auvergne. My research interest is Geometric Representation Theory, which involves studying representation theory through the tools from algebraic geoemtry and from categorification. I am specifically interested in modular representation throy of objects from Lie theory (including Lie algebras, reductive groups, Coxeter groups and their quantizations).

I was graduated from Tsinghua University, under the supervision of Professor Peng Shan.
